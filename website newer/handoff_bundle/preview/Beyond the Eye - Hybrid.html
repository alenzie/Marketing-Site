Beyond the Eye: A New Frontier in Detection

The retina is one of the only places in the body where microvascular and neural structures can be observed directly and noninvasively.

At Ophthalytics, we are advancing the field of oculomics—where retinal imaging extends beyond eye disease to reveal early indicators of systemic health.

By combining AI with multimodal data, we enable earlier risk detection, deeper clinical insights, and more connected care.

This is the future of preventive healthcare—starting with the eye.
