Who We Help

Advancing Eye Care Across Every Stage of the Patient Journey

Ophthalytics supports healthcare providers, researchers, and organizations through AI-powered solutions designed for screening, diagnosis, and clinical research.

From primary care to optometry to pharmaceutical trials, our products are built to address real-world gaps in detection, decision-making, and patient access.

Nsight360

For Primary Care, Population Health, and Screening Programs

Bringing retinal screening to the frontlines of care

Nsight360 is designed for healthcare providers who want to deliver accessible, efficient retinal screening at the point of care. By enabling non-specialists to perform eye exams, it expands access and helps close critical care gaps.

Who it's for

Primary Care Physicians
Endocrinologists & Diabetologists
Nurses & Care Teams
Home Health Agencies
Community Health Centers
Remote & Rural Clinics

How it helps

  • Enables retinal screening without requiring an on-site eye specialist
  • Expands access to underserved populations
  • Supports early detection of diabetic retinopathy
  • Improves workflow efficiency and patient throughput
  • Helps close care gaps and improve quality measures

Nsight360 makes it possible to identify at-risk patients earlier—where they already receive care.

2nd Opinion

For Optometrists and Eye Care Professionals

Enhancing confidence in retinal assessments

2nd Opinion is built for optometrists and eye care providers who want additional support in interpreting retinal images, especially in complex or borderline cases.

Who it's for

Optometrists
Eye Clinics & Optical Chains
Ophthalmology Practices
Reading Centers

How it helps

  • Highlights suspicious regions and potential lesions
  • Supports clinical decision-making in uncertain cases
  • Reduces variability in retinal interpretation
  • Integrates seamlessly into existing workflows

In everyday practice, not every case is clear-cut. 2nd Opinion provides an added layer of insight—helping clinicians see more and decide with greater confidence.

Ophthal360

For High-Volume Screening and Autonomous Deployment

Autonomous AI for scale and reach

Ophthal360 is designed for organizations delivering retinal screening at scale—healthcare systems, large screening programs, and partners serving high-volume markets.

Who it's for

Healthcare Systems
Population Health Programs
Pharmaceutical Companies
Clinical Research Organizations
Imaging Centers
Academic & Research Institutions

How it helps

  • Performs retinal analysis and generates screening outputs without manual interpretation
  • Optimized for large-scale deployment in clinics, screening programs, and population health initiatives
  • Identifies at-risk patients and supports timely referral decisions
  • Accelerates recruitment and enrollment timelines for trials
  • Supports consistent evaluation across sites and geographies

Ophthal360 makes it possible to deliver screening coverage at a scale no specialist-dependent model can match.
