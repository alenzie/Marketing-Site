THE PLATFORM

One Retinal Image. Layered Diagnostic Intelligence.

Ocula360 is our foundational AI engine—designed to detect multiple retinal diseases from a single image. Built on a foundational model architecture, it delivers explainable lesion-level outputs, advanced DRSS scoring, and multimodal compatibility that scales from individual clinic workflows to enterprise screening programs.

From diabetic retinopathy to age-related macular degeneration to glaucoma indicators, Ocula360 transforms one scan into a comprehensive diagnostic signal—so providers can act earlier, researchers can move faster, and patients get the care they need, sooner.

At its core, Ocula360 is not a feature set. It is a platform.

CAPABILITIES

Built for the Full Diagnostic Stack

Multi-Disease Detection

Detects diabetic retinopathy, AMD, glaucoma indicators, vascular abnormalities, hemorrhages, microaneurysms, and exudates—all from a single image.

Automated DRSS Scoring

Standardized severity grading for diabetic retinopathy, supporting consistent disease staging and eligibility decisions across screening programs.

Lesion-Level Explainability

Transparent confidence outputs and lesion overlays show clinicians exactly where and why the model flagged a finding—building trust, not black boxes.

Multimodal & Future-Ready

Compatible with fundus imaging, ultra-widefield (UWF) scans, and patient metadata. Architected to integrate additional inputs as imaging standards evolve.

Nsight360

Nsight360 brings AI-assisted retinal screening directly into primary care, endocrinology, home health, and community clinics—so at-risk patients are identified where they already receive care.

See Who It's For

THE CARE GAP

Millions of at-risk patients never reach a specialist.

Diabetic retinopathy is the leading cause of preventable blindness in working-age adults. It is also one of the most treatable conditions—when caught early.

But specialist shortages, long wait times, and travel barriers mean that millions of at-risk patients never make it in for an eye exam until symptoms appear. By then, intervention is harder and outcomes are worse.

Primary care is where most of these patients already go. It is also where retinal screening has been hardest to deploy. Until now.

THE SOLUTION

Retinal screening built for the front line of care.

Nsight360 enables non-specialists—primary care physicians, nurses, endocrinologists, home health aides—to capture retinal images using accessible hand-held or portable cameras. Our AI analyzes each image and produces a clinically actionable report, reviewed by licensed ophthalmologists, within minutes.

No on-site eye specialist required. No workflow rebuild required. Just faster, broader access to sight-saving screening.
